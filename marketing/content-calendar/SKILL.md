# Content Calendar

## When to Use

Use this skill when the user needs a launch content plan, weekly posting schedule, campaign ideas, or topic backlog.

## Inputs to Collect

- Product or feature being promoted.
- Target audience.
- Main conversion goal.
- Available channels.
- Constraints such as tone, language, cadence, or region.

## Workflow

1. Define the audience and primary message.
2. Split ideas across awareness, education, proof, and conversion.
3. Create a one-week or two-week calendar.
4. Include channel, format, hook, and CTA for each item.
5. Keep recommendations realistic for a small team.

## Output Format

```markdown
| Day | Channel | Format | Hook | CTA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Mon | ... | ... | ... | ... |
```
